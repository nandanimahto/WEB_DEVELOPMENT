PERSONAL TRAINER

A personal trainer in a gym is a fitness professional who works with individuals to help them achieve their fitness goals. Personal trainers provide personalized exercise plans, instruction, and motivation to help clients improve their physical fitness, lose weight, build muscle, and enhance overall health and well-being.
